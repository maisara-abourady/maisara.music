Philosophy
Music is architecture in time — every set a journey through tension and release, ancient and synthetic, familiar and unknown.

I approach each session as a curator of emotional states, not a performer of tracks. The warmup is not the opening act — it is the foundation. That space before the night fully reveals itself is where the mood is shaped, where energy is coaxed rather than forced, where the room decides what it wants to become.

Progressive house, for me, is not a genre. It is a discipline. A commitment to patience. To trusting that the journey matters more than any single moment within it.
